General Information

Oakbrook neighborhood is located in the eastern suburb of Louisville. It is quiet and beautiful. It is a great place for the families who are looking for quality life and education.

Schools

Many schools provide quality education near Oakbrook Neighborhood. Chancey Elementary School is in the walking distance to the neighborhood.

Shopping

Costco, Kroger, Target, Aldi, Walmart and a few shopping malls are nearby.

Restaurants

A lot of restaurants serving delicious Italian, Mexican, Asian, European, and American food are close to the neighborhood.

Highlight

Oakbrook neighborhood is quiet and beautiful. There are many good schools nearby. Shopping is very convenient. You will really enjoy the delicious food if you live in this neighborhood.
